## Earth Day!

By Megna Prakash (8Ti)

Wednesday, April 22nd was Earth Day. Earth Day promotes awareness about the environment and celebrates nature and the Earth. On campus, Peer Advisory and the admin gifted different types of plants to all of the AE teachers to celebrate Earth Day. The first Earth Day was held in 1970 to protest for an oil spill. Shortly following the creation of Earth Day, the EPA(Environmental Protection Agency) was established. Some ways that you can celebrate this day are by practicing the 3Rs, reduce, reuse, and recycle and learn more about the wonderful nature present on Earth . All it takes is small steps to make the Earth a better place.  Some small actions you can take to improve your environmental footprint are using reusable water bottles and bags, saving water, and walking to school if possible.

## “What kind of Recycler are you” Quiz

1. At the end of the year, your notebook has over 20 pages left. What do you do?
 A: Rip out unused pages to use as scrap paper and recycle the notebook
 B: Keep the notebook even if you won’t use it much
 C: Throw the whole notebook into the trash
 D: Save it and intentionally use all remaining pages later for doodling

2.  The cashier hands you 50 napkins for one order of fries, what do you do with the extra napkins?
 A: Take a few to use for later, and put the rest in the trash can
 B: Take the entire stack and put it in your bag even though you won’t use it
 C: Throw the entire stack in the recycling
 D: Hand most of them back to the cashier

3. Your favorite 0.5mm gel pen runs out of ink. What do you do?
 A: Keep the cap or clicker as a spare and throw the rest in the trash
 B: Keep it even if its broken
 C: You toss it into the trash can/recycling(whatever’s closest)
 D: You have a refillable ink pen, so you switch out the cartridges

4. A teacher hands out a long packet that has already been posted on schoology. What do you do?
 A: Use the hard copy for class and recycle it later
 B: Stick it in your folder until the end of the year
 C: Use the packet for doodling or folding into origami 
 D: Access it digitally and save the clutter

5. You are studying for an exam/comps/precomps. What describes your style?
 A: You do a mix of digital and manual studying and recycle the paper after the the test
 B: You handwrite every single note in a notebook to save, and make manual flashcards
 C: You take notes and solve problems on loose leaf paper and trash them after the test. 
 D: You go totally digital. You type out your notes and make flashcards on Quizlet

RESULTS:

Mostly As: conscious reuser, and you try to recycle/reuse whenever it’s convenient
Mostly Bs: Just-in-case collector: You keep extra items, even if they don’t get used
Mostly Cs: One-and-Done You usually discard items because it’s convenient. 
Mostly Ds: The low-waste lifestyle: You prioritize minimizing unnecessary waste by using reusable items
