## Spring Break Highlights

By Stephanie Song (8Pt)

Three weeks ago, students went on spring break. Some flew across the globe, exploring the beautiful architecture of southern Spain, strolling in the streets of Japan, or watching an immersive 4D version of The Wizard of Oz at the Sphere. Others stayed home, catching up on sleep or watching TV in the comfort of their own home. Whether they went on a trip or stayed home, each student had a unique highlight.
Ethan Fan (7Mn), who went to Tokyo, Osaka, and Kyoto, said his “favorite part of the trip was the hot bathtubs. :) ”
Meng Sun (4Ge) watched The Wizard of Oz at the Sphere in Las Vegas. Her favorite part was “when apples dropped from the ceiling.” Although she didn’t get an apple, she recommends sitting in rows 200-300 for the best chances of getting one.
Helena Chen (9) noted that “Spanish cuisine is so good!” and her favorite dishes were “paella, tapas, and oxtail.”
Back in Washington, Jeremy Dai (10) went to the RTC Exotics meet with his friends, Ruben Jing (10) and Alan Zhang (9), where they saw unique cars. He was glad he was able to “share his passion with classmates like Ruben and Alan.”
No matter the destination, we hope you had a good spring break and got some well-deserved rest.
