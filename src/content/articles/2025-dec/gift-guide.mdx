## Gift Guide

By Clara Jeon 8Ti

Unsure of what to get your friends and family for holiday gifts this year? Here are some ideas to get you going!

-stuffed animals
-board games
-books
-blankets
-fuzzy socks
-candy
-headphones/earbuds
-keychains/small trinkets
-stationary
-LEGO sets
-DIY craft kits
-merch (sports, TV show, etc.)
-vinyl

Look for meaningful gifts that the recipient will actually enjoy or use!
