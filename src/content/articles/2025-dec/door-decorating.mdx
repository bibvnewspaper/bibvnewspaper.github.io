## Door Decorating contest

By Molly Miao (8Ti)

This month the teachers here at Basis Independent Bellevue have participated in a festive door decorating competition! When walking around campus, you may have noticed these brightly adorned doors, often featuring fun and colorful images. Several students have helped the teachers decorate for this event, such as Mia L. 
“I decorated Ms. Iliescu and Ms. PV's door during student hours. It was really fun, knowing that you were a part of the final decoration that everyone outside can see. I didn't do much, but I cut out the background for the door and taped it on (with Ms. PV's help). I guess in a way it was like the foundation for the whole thing,” she remarks.
The winners of this year’s contest have already come out, namely Ms. Illiescu and Ms, PV, Ms. Xie, and Ms. Zhang and Ms. Chen.
